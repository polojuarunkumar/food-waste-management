Help & FAQs

How to donate food?
You can donate food by visiting our Donation page and filling out the food pickup request form.
How will my donation be used?
All food donations are inspected, sorted, and distributed to shelters and organizations helping people in need.
What should I do if my food donation is near or past its expiration date?
Please check our guidelines. Expired food is usually not accepted for safety reasons, but some items may still be usable.
